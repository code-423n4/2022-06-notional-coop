christn | IndexCoop 06/10/2022 10:51 PM
Generally the scope of the contest is limited to the NotionalTradeModule. I'd say that the dependencies are only in scope as far as their interaction / usage by the NotionalTradeModule is concerned. I.e. if you find an issue in the NotionalTradeModule that stems from it's interaction with any of these dependencies then that'd be in scope whereas an issue in these dependencies that does not affect the NotionalTradeModule would not be. (if you do find such an issue you might want to take a look at set protocols bug bounty program though: https://docs.tokensets.com/protocol/security#bug-bounty). Also those dependencies are not in scope for the gas-optimization. Most of these dependencies have been audited by OZ in their set protocol audit: https://blog.openzeppelin.com/set-protocol-audit/#:~:text=Set%20Protocol%20provides%20a%20mechanism,goal%20but%20changes%20the%20architecture. (edited)
